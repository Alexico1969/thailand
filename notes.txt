<h3>VISA:</h3>
-------
Steps for the non-immigrant 'O' VISA: 

 -> have $ 23,000 in a US / EU bank account
 -> 90 day visa (get through Thai Embassy in your country)
 -> open a Thai bank account
 -> deposit $ 23,000
 -> get proof of residence
 -> Extend the 90-day visa for 12 months
