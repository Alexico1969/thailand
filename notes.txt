<h3>VISA:</h3>
<hr>
<p>Steps for the Non-Immigrant 'O' Visa:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Have $23,000 in a US/EU bank account.</li>
  <li>Apply for a 90-day visa (through the Thai Embassy in your country).</li>
  <li>Open a Thai bank account.</li>
  <li>Deposit $23,000 into your Thai bank account.</li>
  <li>Get proof of residence.</li>
  <li>Extend the 90-day visa to 12 months.</li>
</ul>

<h3>PROS/CONS per city</h3>
<hr>

<h4>PATTAYA</h4>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Pros:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Many condos for rent/sale</li>
      <li>Food (many restaurants)</li>
      <li>Beaches / close to islands</li>
      <li>Less rain</li>
      <li>Geared towards older people / retirees</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>Cons:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Pretty hot</li>
      <li>Prices fluctuate during high/low season</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>

<h4>BANGKOK</h4>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Pros:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Excellent transportation (in the city and around Thailand)</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>Cons:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Crazy traffic</li>
      <li>Poor air quality</li>
      <li>Rains a lot</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>

<h4>PHUKET</h4>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Pros:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Easy to access</li>
      <li>Best beaches</li>
      <li>Easy access to Indonesia</li>
      <li>Catered somewhat to Westerners</li>
      <li>Fewer expats (beaches are less crowded)</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>Cons:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>A bit more expensive</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>
